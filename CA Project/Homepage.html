About Us

Women's Hockey Ireland, also know as WHI, is an organisation that encourages women and young girls to play hockey in Ireland. We are the official website for the Irish Women's Hockey Team and work with Hockey Ireland to make sure that women and young girls have the same opportunities on the field.

We hold training camps and help our female hockey players to unlock their full potential to become great athletes.

The purpose of our website is to encourage and inspire our hockey players and any aspiring hockey players. We have all of the relevant information that is needed for hockey matches, such as rules, results and clubs involved.
