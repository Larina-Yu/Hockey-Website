EY Hockey League


The EY Hockey League was first played in the Hockey Season of 2008-2009. It has always featured 10 teams from both the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland. Loreto Hockey Club were the first winners of the League, however UCD and Railway Union are tied for the most wins, at three wins each.
